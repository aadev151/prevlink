prevlink.js

Just let your users preview any link on your webpage



Quick start

First of all, add these three lines to the end of your body. This is prevlink.js and its dependencies.


<script src="https://unpkg.com/@popperjs/core@2"></script>
<script src="https://unpkg.com/tippy.js@6"></script>
<script src="https://unpkg.com/prevlink"></script>

Attention! The order matters!


Then, you need to setup the links you want your users to preview. To do this, just add three attributes to a tag. pltitle sets the title of a link, pldesc sets the description, and plimg the image for the link. All the attributes are optional, so you can have just an image or no preview at all if you wish.
